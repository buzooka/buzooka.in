### Method: Manual Request

If you cannot access your account, please send an email to **vishnu@buzooka.in** from the email address associated with your account.

**Template for Email Request:**

> **Subject:** Formal Data Deletion Request
>
> I request that Biolocal Technologies (OPC) Pvt Ltd permanently delete all personal data and technical credentials associated with the account: [Insert Email Address].
>
> I understand that this action is irreversible and will terminate my access to the Buzooka platform.

## What Data is Deleted?

Upon a deletion request, we purge the following from our active databases:

- **Personal Identity Information:** Name, Email, and Profile Picture.
- **Technical Credentials:** All encrypted Secret Keys and Tokens (GitHub, AWS, Vercel, etc.).
- **Active Sessions:** All current login tokens.

## What Data is Retained?

We are legally required to retain certain data for financial and audit purposes:

- **Transaction Records:** Records of payments handled by Lemon Squeezy are retained for tax and regulatory compliance in India.
- **Support Logs:** Communications sent to our support email may be archived for legal record-keeping.
